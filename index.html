How This Switch Works

If the switch is off then it means that it will seperate text with spaces by the entered amount.
If the switch is on then it means that it will seperate text with line breaks by the entered amount.
One need to enter the amount and then toogle the switch to apply changes.
For e.g : Entering 2 and then toogling the switch to 'on' will sperates lines with 2 breaks . Entering 2 and then toogling the switch to 'off' will seperate lines with 2 spaces
